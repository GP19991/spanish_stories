Spanish Stories by CEFR Level

Welcome!

This website offers free Spanish stories organized by CEFR level (A1 to B2). Whether you're a beginner or intermediate learner, you'll find stories tailored to your skills.

Each story includes:

  • Clear, simple language for your level.
  • English translations (line by line).
  • Learning objectives (grammar, vocabulary).

What is CEFR? → Learn about the CEFR levels here

Why This Website?

I created this site because I struggled to find free, engaging Spanish stories matched to my level. Most resources were either too difficult or too childish. I wanted realistic, everyday stories to practice reading and listening without frustration.

Now, you can:

  • Read stories at your exact CEFR level.
  • Listen to natural Spanish (audio coming soon!).
  • Learn vocabulary in context.
